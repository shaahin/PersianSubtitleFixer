This app converts text encoding of your Persian (Farsi) or Arabic .srt subtitle files to UTF-8 so they can be used in almost every video player.
Supports processing multiple subtitles at once!
Just drag and drop your .srt subtitle files into the app; They'll be converted and saved automatically.
